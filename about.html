About BABISHA

Connecting fabric buyers with trusted suppliers worldwide, delivering quality and sustainability in every thread.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, BABISHA began as a small initiative to bridge the gap between fabric buyers and suppliers. What started as a passion project has grown into a global platform trusted by thousands of customers worldwide.

Our mission is simple: to provide access to the finest fabrics while ensuring ethical sourcing, quality assurance, and sustainable practices throughout our supply chain.

Today, we work with over 200 certified suppliers across 25 countries, offering a diverse range of fabrics from traditional cotton to luxury silk, all while maintaining our commitment to environmental responsibility and fair trade practices.
